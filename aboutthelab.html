About the lab

研究兴趣

目前，我们的兴趣主要在以下几个方面：

（1）自然界生物并非孤立存在，而是存在复杂的相互作用，包括捕食、竞争、互惠等。我们希望通过描述或收集自然界及人类干扰生态系统中的物种互作关系（如种子传播、食物网等），解析生态互作网络的结构、功能、进化、时空动态及其之间的联系，进一步推论其在应对气候变化、生态安全与保护的作用。研究手段包括理论模型、计算机模拟、数据挖掘、野外采样与调查、统计模型等。

（2）目前，关于生态互作网络的理论层出不穷，但多数尚未经过实验验证。因此，我们希望通过设计简单的室内动植物种群或行为实验，验证生态互作网络相关理论。

（3）当前，气候变化及人类活动对生物多样保护及灾害防治带来了巨大的挑战。我们希望通过野外调查及数据挖掘以获取动物种群及群落、生物事件的时空数据，采用统计模型分析其时空规律以及全球气候、人类活动的影响。

Research interests

At present, our research interests lie in the following directions：

（1）In nature, organisms are not isolated but interact with each other, forming predation, competition, mutualism, etc. We hope to describe or collect species interactions in natural and human-disturbed ecosystems, analyze the structure, function, evolution, spatial-temporal dynamics and their relationships. From that, we may infer the implications of species interactions in climate change, ecological conservation, etc. For those purposes, we applied a variety of methods including theoretical modelling, computer simulation, data mining, field survey, and statistical modeling.

（2）Currently, there are a lot of theories or hypotheses for ecological networks and community dynamics, but without testing. We hope to test some of them by designing indoor experimental systems.

（3）Climate change and human disturbance have brought great challenges for biodiversity conservation and combating biological disasters. We hope to compile spatial and temporal data from field surveys and literature, and know the general patterns and influencing factors.
